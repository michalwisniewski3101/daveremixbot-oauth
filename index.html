DaveRemixBot

DaveRemixBot is a private operator dashboard for preparing ready-made short videos, reviewing platform-specific captions, and approving publishing jobs.

The application is controlled by the content owner. It does not generate fake engagement, automated likes, spam comments, or actions intended to bypass platform limits.

Public pages on this site are used only for app information, policy documents, and OAuth redirect handling. Application secrets and publishing controls are stored in the private local DaveRemixBot instance.
